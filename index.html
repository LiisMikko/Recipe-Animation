Tomato Soup

    • Cooks in 45 minutes
    • Easy to make
    • Healthy
Ingredients Amount
Carrots 2
Celery 2 sticks
Onions 2
Garlic 2 gloves
Vegetable stock cubes 2
Canned tomatoes 800g
Olive oil 1 tbsp
  1. Peel and shop the vegetables.
  2. Cook the prepped vegetables in olive oil.
  3. Add stock cubes, boiling water and canned tomatoes.
  4. Boil the soup.
  5. Add seasoning and use a blender to pulse the soup until smooth.
